Panditya Hub

What we do ?

  1. Organize and conduct Pujas, Vastu Shanti, Special Pujas, Havanas upon request.
  2. Organize and conduct yajnas and ceremonies for universal peace and well being.
  3. Organize chanting of Vedas by scholars on an ongoing basis for universal peace and well being.
  4. Propagate the high moral values contained in Vedas and Hindu religious scriptures to enable people to lead a righteous and meritorious life and become good citizens.
  5. Assist public in spiritual upliftment by educating them about the methods of spiritual sadhana and practices.
  6. Educate, encourage and assist people to perform pujas, religious festivals, Vedic rituals and yajnas.
  7. Organize religious and spiritual discourses, bhajans, keertans, mass prayers and mass ceremonies such as marriage and upanayanam.
  8. Produce and broadcast programs pertaining to religion and spirituality through television and other means of communication such as social media and webcasting.
